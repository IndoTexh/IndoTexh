Welcome to American Citizen Services (ACS) for Phnom Penh, Cambodia. We provide a range of services
and information. All non-emergency consular services require an appointment. We will not accept walk-
ins. This assists us in providing prompt and efficient consular services to American Citizens residing in
Cambodia. Please plan accordingly.
